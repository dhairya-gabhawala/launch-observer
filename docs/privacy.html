Launch Observer Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 10, 2026

Launch Observer is a browser extension that helps users inspect analytics and marketing network calls. This policy explains what data the extension handles and how it is used.

Data We Collect

Launch Observer does not collect, transmit, or sell personal data. All inspection happens locally in your browser.

Data Processed Locally

To provide the product features, the extension processes the following data only on your device:

  • Network request metadata and payloads for domains you explicitly allow.
  • Session names, site labels, and request history stored in browser local storage.

Data Sharing

No data is sent to external servers. The extension operates entirely offline after installation.

Permissions

Launch Observer requests permissions only to observe network requests, associate them with tabs, and remember your settings locally.

Contact

If you have questions, contact the maintainer listed in the repository or store listing.
